Yan Yu

A Rough Introduction:

Yan Yu, born in Beijing on May 26, 1997, is a female singer and actress in Mainland China. In November 2015, she released her first personal mini album "Bad Girl". In June 2016, she participated in Dragon TV's inspirational program "Come on! Beautiful Girl" and won. In August 2018, she released her first solo album "Twilight"; in the same month, she starred in her first film and television work "Cute Pets Enter the Mortal Master"; in December, she starred in the trilogy of action movies "The Return of Special Forces". In March 2020, she participated in the iQiyi inspirational program "Youth with You Season 2", received a "3A" rating, and joined THE9 group with a 4th place finish in the finals; in December, she participated in the lyrics of her personal single "Rose". "The Peak" was released with the group's album. In January 2021, she became the cover character of the first issue of the world fashion garden ELLE electronic magazine; in April, she was awarded the title of "One Belt and One Road Folk-Friendly Youth Representative"; in the same month, she was awarded the title of "Outstanding Youth for Rural Revitalization"; on December 5, officially graduated from THE9 group. In January 2022, she won the "New Singer of the Year" award from the China Central Radio and Television Station's CCTV Dream and Xinhua Media's "2021 Global Chinese Music Awards Ceremony"; in February, she participated in the singing of the single "The Nameless Distance" as a support for the Winter Olympic theme song released. In May, her personal mini-album "W" containing all her original songs was released. In May 2023, she released her personal EP "WORLD (Mine)" and officially announced the start of her first solo tour "WORLD (Mine)"; in September, she was awarded the title of "Gannan Prefecture Publicity Ambassador". In 2023, she won the leap female artist of the year at the 2023 Asian Music Festival.
